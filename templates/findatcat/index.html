[DATE]…The financial world is in turmoil; the economic forecasts are beyond grim. Unemployment rates are among the highest seen in decades. Millions of Americans are estimated to be “underwater” in their mortgages—owing more on their homes than their properties are worth. Retirement funds are dwindling. The threat of a double dip recession looms.

In the midst of all this tumult, a person seeking simple information about aspects of their financial life is often thwarted. While the federal government maintains vast treasure troves of relevant financial data, and wields the power to compel corporate America to report much more, not all of this information is disclosed.

This page serves as hub for our reporting on financial data—who has it, why it is important, whether it is publicly available and whether it’s in a format that is easily accessible. You will find stories, links to databases and other resources.
