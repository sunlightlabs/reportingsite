    Super PAC spending and fundraising by political orientation, 2011-12

    Totals on this page only reflect money raised and spent by Super PACs, which only reflect a part of the outside spending picture. For a more complete picture see the outside spending overview page.

    Political orientation as used on this page is determined by researchers at The Sunlight Foundation based on a review of spending patterns and public communications--it does not reflect any official designation. In general, a conservative approach was used; if a PAC didn't fit neatly into party labels a label has not been applied. For more information see the methodology. The categorization currently used is available in the 'political orientation' column of a downloadable csv file summarizing Super PACs.
